What's here

Phuket is a small island with a very big story. People have been arriving here for more than a thousand years — by sea, by river, and lately by plane — and each group has left a mark you can still find today.

Two pieces are ready to read. Open either one and the rest of the chapter unfolds on the page.

How to use this book

Words with a dotted red line under them are clickable — tap or click to see a short definition. Each piece also has a map or visual you can interact with. The book works on a phone or a laptop.
